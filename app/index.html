Features

Everything, connected

Every part of your shop talks to every other part — quote a job, convert it to an invoice, track it on the board, and watch your numbers update live.

🧾

Invoicing & Estimates

Build estimates with line items and photos, send them for approval, then convert to invoices in one click. Branded, exportable PDFs every time.

🛒

Point of Sale

A real checkout flow for walk-ins — categorized items, custom line items, discounts, cash drawer with change calculation, and instant receipts.

🔧

Jobs Board

A Trello-style board for production — assign team members, track checklists, set priorities and due dates, see overdue jobs at a glance.

👥

CRM

Every client's full history — total spend, job count, contact info — searchable and editable, with archive instead of delete to protect your records.

📊

Analytics

Revenue by day, week, month or year. Top clients. Sales by service category. Know exactly where your money is coming from.

🔐

Roles & Permissions

Google sign-in with no passwords to manage. Give your installer access to the jobs board only, your bookkeeper access to finances only.
