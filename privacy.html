Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how B2B Print Hub ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our business management application.

1. Who this applies to

B2B Print Hub is an internal business management tool. Access is restricted to approved team members and is not available for public sign-up. This policy covers information collected from approved users of the application.

2. Information we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google account profile picture (if available)

We do not request or receive access to your Gmail, Google Drive, Google Calendar, or any other Google service beyond basic identity verification.

Within the application itself, authorized users may enter and store:

  • Customer/client contact information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Invoice, estimate, and job records related to business operations
  • Photos uploaded for job documentation or site inspections
  • Activity logs reflecting actions taken within the app

3. How we use information

Information is used solely to operate the application's core functionality: authenticating approved users, managing invoices and estimates, tracking jobs, maintaining customer records, and generating business reports for the organization using the app.

4. How information is stored

Data is stored using Supabase, a hosted PostgreSQL database provider. Access to the database is restricted to authenticated, approved users via Row Level Security policies. We do not sell, rent, or share stored data with third parties.

5. Data retention

Business records (invoices, clients, jobs, estimates) are retained for as long as the organization using this application requires them for business and recordkeeping purposes. Authorized administrators may archive or delete records at their discretion.

6. Your choices

If you are an approved user and wish to have your account access revoked, contact your administrator. If you have questions about specific data stored about you or a client, contact the organization operating this instance of the application.

7. Third-party services

This application uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google OAuth — for sign-in authentication only
  • Supabase — for secure data storage

Each of these providers has its own privacy practices governing the data they process on our behalf.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to the administrator of your B2B Print Hub instance.
